🎮 ISRAEL WINS — ROAR OF THE LION 🦁
 
A quick mobile-first game built with Claude Code! No frameworks, no bloat — just pure HTML5 canvas magic built in minutes.
 
✨ GAME FEATURES:
💕 Dual-touch controller system — drag the left side to aim, tap the right to shoot love
🎯 Send positive vibes (good emojis) and dodge negativity (bad emojis)  
🔥 Progressive difficulty — the challenge intensifies every 10 seconds
❤️ Fill the LOVE meter to 1000 and bring SHALOM!
🌐 Bilingual support — English & Hebrew with full RTL/LTR handling
📱 Fully responsive — optimized for landscape mobile, tablets, and desktop
 
🎮 HOW TO PLAY:
→ Left side drag = Move up & down to position yourself
→ Right side tap = Fire love projectiles at the enemy
→ Catch the good emojis (💕🌸🦄🐾😊🌈⭐🎀)
→ Avoid the bad ones (💩😡💣🔴)
→ Reach 1000 love points to WIN!
